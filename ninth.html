News Report

A 15 year old highschool student hijacks local radio signals to play Rick Astley’s Never gonna give you up. 

  
            A 15 year old student who recently moved from Nova Scotia indulges in “a little bit of trolling” he states. 
        He built a small contraption that can broadcast any music over a radio signal in a small area. He uses this 
        to play Never gonna give you up on the school bus’ radio and unsuspecting traffic nearby. This is known to many 
        people as a "Rickroll". A Rickroll is an internet meme involving pranking an unsuspecting victim with the 1987 
        song "Never Gonna Give You Up" performed by Rick Astley.

            There isn’t much harm in this project currently, however, furthering the project could result in legal issues 
        and a potential nuisance to surrounding neighborhoods.
